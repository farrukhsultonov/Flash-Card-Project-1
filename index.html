How to play Flash Card

Flash cards game requires player to match 2 cards of same picture to continue. there will be 12 total cards or 6 pair.

The player clicks card to show image, one at a time. If the cards match, players continues to find rest of pairs. Play continues until all the cards have been matched. You should watch carefully and remember where cards are located. Then, when you click again, you may remember where to find a matching card.

If the cards do not match, the card flips to its original state, and player tries again.
